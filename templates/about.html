What Makes Our Coffee Special?

At Bean & Bliss, we believe coffee is more than a drink - it's an experience. Founded in 2020 by Ravi Ji on Mandla Road, Tilhari, we source our beans directly from Chikmagalur hills.

Every cup is brewed with love and served with a smile. We offer free WiFi, cozy seating, and a pet-friendly environment.

☕ Kaise Shuru Hua?

Bean & Bliss Café ki shuruwat 2020 mein Ravi Ji ne ki thi. Unka sapna tha Jabalpur mein ek aisi jagah banana jahan log sirf coffee na piye, balki ek experience le kar jayein.

Hum Mandla Road, Tilhari mein located hain. Ye jagah isliye choose ki kyunki yahan ka environment peaceful hai - padhai karne, kaam karne, ya doston ke saath time bitane ke liye perfect!

🌱 Hum Kya Alag Karte Hain?

  • Direct Sourcing: Hum apne coffee beans seedha Chikmagalur ke farmers se lete hain. No middleman, matlab fresh aur authentic quality.
  • Small Batch Roasting: Har week fresh beans roast karte hain. Purani coffee kabhi serve nahi karte.
  • Handcrafted: Har cup ko barista pyaar se banata hai. Machine nahi, insaan banata hai!
  • Community Space: Free WiFi, charging points, comfortable seating - sab kuch hai. Students aur freelancers ka favorite spot.

🎯 Hamara Mission

Jabalpur ke logon ko world-class coffee affordable price mein dena. Saath mein ek aisi community banana jahan sab comfortable feel karein.

🐕 Pet Friendly Café

Haan! Aap apne dogs ko bhi la sakte ho. Hum unke liye paani ka bowl free mein dete hain. Kyunki pets bhi family hain! ❤️
